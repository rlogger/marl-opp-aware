## Publication Guardrails

Run:

```bash
mopa-check-results
```

Expected checks:

- BC recovers at least 75% of MAPPO's edge over random.
- Latent-conditioned BC beats vanilla by at least one action-accuracy point in
  the observation sweep and reaches the oracle accuracy band.
- Oracle placement recovers at least 70% of the deployed pooled-BC to MAPPO gap.
- The current unsupervised deployed latent is allowed to be modest, but it must
  not materially hurt deployed captures.
